DRAGON BALL


SYNOPSIS:

Dragon Ball tells the tale of a young warrior by the name of Son Goku, a young peculiar boy with a tail who embarks on a quest to become stronger and learns of the Dragon Balls, when, once all 7 are gathered, grant any wish of choice.



AUTHOR:

Japanese "Mangaka" or manga artist Akira Toriyama (Manga is a comic created in Japan or in Japanese language, drawn in a specific late 19th century Japanese style). His most well-known work is the manga series "Dragon Ball," which is among the most read and lucrative in the entire world. He is a well-known character designer for video games, having worked on games like the "Dragon Quest" series and "Blue Dragon," among others. He was born and reared in Japan, where he also discovered his talent for manga art in the classroom. He left his position as an advertising executive after a few years to pursue a career as a manga artist. After publishing a few manga, he attained real fame when his comedic series "Dr. Slump was well-liked by many. Millions of copies of his manga were sold all over the world, and he followed this up with even greater success with the "Dragon Ball" series. Both of these manga were turned into anime. Later, he produced one-shots and shorter manga. In appreciation of his outstanding artistic talent, he won prestigious accolades. He is married to Yoshimi Kato, with whom he has a daughter and a son.
